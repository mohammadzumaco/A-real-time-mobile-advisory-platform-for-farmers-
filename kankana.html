1. Land Preparation

Choose good, loose, and well-drained soil. Clear the field from weeds, stones, and debris before planting watermelon. Surround the field with trees or fences to protect the crop from pests and animals.

2. Za…ìin Iri

2. Seed Selection

Select watermelon seeds suitable for the climate and irrigation, especially those resistant to diseases and pests. Good seeds help increase yield and improve fruit quality.

3. Shuka Kankana

A shuka kankana a cikin ramuka, a bar nisan kusan 60-90cm tsakanin tsire-tsire da 1.5-2m tsakanin layuka. Ban ruwa bayan shuka sosai don ∆ôasa ta yi danshi sosai. Ban ruwa na farko ya kamata a yi nan da kwanaki 3-5 bayan shuka.

3. Planting Watermelon

Plant watermelon seeds in holes spaced about 60-90cm apart and 1.5-2 meters between rows. Water thoroughly after planting to moisten the soil well. The first watering should be done within 3-5 days after planting.

4. Yin Amfani da Taki

Yi amfani da taki mai nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), da potassium (K) kamar NPK 15-15-15 kafin shuka da kuma lokacin girma don ∆ôara yawan kankana da ∆ôarfafa tsiro.

4. Fertilizer Application

Use fertilizers containing nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), and potassium (K), such as NPK 15-15-15 before planting and during growth stages to increase watermelon yield and strengthen growth.

5. Kulawa da Ciyawa

A cire ciyawa da ke hana kankana samun hasken rana ta hanyar noman hannu ko amfani da magungunan kashe ciyawa masu dacewa. Yi wannan aiki akai-akai don hana ciyawar shuka da haifar da matsaloli.

5. Weed Control

Remove weeds that block sunlight from the watermelon plants through manual weeding or appropriate herbicides. Perform this regularly to prevent weed growth and problems.

6. Kula da Kwari da Cututtuka

A lura da kwari kamar su ∆ôwari, da cututtuka kamar ∆ôura da ∆ôwayoyin cuta. Idan aka ga tsire-tsire na nuna alamun cuta, cire su nan take don hana yaduwa. Yi amfani da magungunan kashe kwari da cututtuka masu dacewa a lokacin da ya dace.

6. Pest and Disease Management

Watch out for pests such as beetles and diseases like powdery mildew and fungi. Remove infected plants immediately to prevent spread. Use suitable pesticides and fungicides at appropriate times.

7. Ban Ruwa Mai Inganci

A yi ban ruwa akai-akai musamman a lokacin zafi da bushewa. Fara ban ruwa bayan kwanaki 3-5 daga shuka. Kada a yi ban ruwa mai yawa don kada ∆ôasa ta yi ruwa sosai. Yin amfani da tsarin drip irrigation zai taimaka wajen adana ruwa da inganta shuka.

7. Efficient Irrigation

Water regularly, especially during hot and dry periods. Start watering 3-5 days after planting. Avoid overwatering to prevent waterlogging. Using drip irrigation helps conserve water and improve plant growth.

8. Girbi da Ajiya

Kankana yana girma cikin kwanaki 70-90. A girbe lokacin da kankana ya yi cikakken girma, ya yi laushi, kuma ya zama ja sosai a ciki. A ajiye a wuri mai sanyi da bushewa don kiyaye kankana daga lalacewa.

8. Harvesting and Storage

Watermelon matures in 70-90 days. Harvest when the fruit is fully grown, soft, and deeply red inside. Store in a cool, dry place to prevent spoilage.
