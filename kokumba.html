1. Land Preparation

Choose good, loose, warm soil with good drainage. Cucumber requires fertile soil rich in nutrients and proper drainage to avoid waterlogging. Clear the field of weeds and debris before planting.

2. Za…ìin Iri

Za…ìi iri na kokumba wa…óanda suka dace da yanayin ku. Ana samun iri masu tsire tsawo da kuma wa…óanda suke shuka gajere. Za…ìi iri mai jure cututtuka da kwari don samun amfanin gona mai kyau.

2. Seed Selection

Select cucumber varieties suitable for your climate. There are long-vining and bush types. Choose seeds resistant to pests and diseases for a better yield.

3. Shuka Kokumba

A shuka iri a cikin layuka ko ramuka, a bar nisan kusan 30-45cm tsakanin tsire-tsire da 90-120cm tsakanin layuka. Ana iya fara shuka a cikin masaka sannan a dasa a waje bayan tsiro.

3. Planting Cucumber

Plant seeds in rows or holes, spacing 30-45cm between plants and 90-120cm between rows. Seeds can be started in nurseries and transplanted outdoors after seedlings develop.

4. Yin Amfani da Taki

Yi amfani da taki mai nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), da potassium (K) musamman NPK 15-15-15 ko taki mai kyau a lokacin shuka da girma don ∆ôara yawan amfanin gona. Ana iya amfani da taki na zamani ko taki na gargajiya kamar taki na dabbobi.

4. Fertilizer Application

Use fertilizers containing nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), and potassium (K), especially NPK 15-15-15 or organic manure at planting and during growth to increase yield. Modern or organic fertilizers can be used.

5. Ban Ruwa (Irrigation)

5. Irrigation

Cucumber requires regular watering, especially during dry periods. Water 2-3 times per week or use drip irrigation to conserve water and maintain soil moisture.

6. Kare Tsire-Tsire daga Kwari da Cututtuka

Kula da kwari kamar su aphids, caterpillars, da cututtuka kamar ∆ôurar farin (powdery mildew). Yi amfani da magungunan kashe kwari da fungicides masu dacewa, sannan a cire tsire-tsire masu cutarwa don hana yaduwa.

6. Pest and Disease Control

Monitor for pests like aphids, caterpillars, and diseases such as powdery mildew. Use appropriate pesticides and fungicides, and remove infected plants promptly to prevent spread.

7. Girbi da Ajiye Kokumba

Kokumba na shirya girbi bayan kwanaki 50 zuwa 70 daga shuka. A yanke kokumba yayin da suke da girman da ya dace, a kula kada su yi tsufa a cikin gona. A ajiye su a wuri mai sanyi da iska mai kyau domin tsawaita rayuwa.

7. Harvesting and Storage

Cucumbers are ready to harvest 50 to 70 days after planting. Pick them when they reach appropriate size, avoid over-maturity in the field. Store in a cool, well-ventilated place to extend shelf life.
