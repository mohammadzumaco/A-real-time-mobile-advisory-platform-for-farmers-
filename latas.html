1. Land Preparation

Choose good, loose, and well-drained soil. Clear the field from weeds, stones, and debris before planting kale. Fence the field or use trees to protect from animals and pests.

2. Za…ìin Iri

Za…ìi iri na latas wa…óanda suka dace da yanayi da ban ruwa, musamman wa…óanda suka jure cututtuka da kwari. Iri mai kyau yana taimaka wa amfanin gona ya yi yawa kuma lafiyayye.

2. Seed Selection

Select kale seeds suitable for the climate and irrigation, especially those resistant to pests and diseases. Good seeds help increase yield and ensure healthy crops.

3. Shuka Latas

A shuka latas a cikin ramuka ko layuka. Bar nisan kusan 30cm tsakanin tsire-tsire da 50-60cm tsakanin layuka. Ban ruwa sosai bayan shuka don ∆ôasa ta sami danshi mai kyau.

3. Planting Kale

Plant kale seeds in holes or rows. Leave about 30cm between plants and 50-60cm between rows. Water thoroughly after planting to ensure the soil is moist.

4. Yin Amfani da Taki

Yi amfani da taki mai nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), da potassium (K) kamar NPK 15-15-15 kafin shuka da kuma lokacin girma don ∆ôara yawan latas da ∆ôarfafa tsiro.

4. Fertilizer Application

Use fertilizers containing nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), and potassium (K), such as NPK 15-15-15 before planting and during growth to increase kale yield and strengthen growth.

5. Ban Ruwa a Tsarin LATAS (Irrigation)

Yi ban ruwa na yau da kullum, musamman a lokacin zafi da bushewa. Ban ruwa na tsawon minti 30 zuwa 60 a kowace rana ko bisa bukata. Amfani da tsarin drip irrigation ko ban ruwa mai sau∆ôi zai taimaka wajen kiyaye ruwa da inganta girma.

5. Irrigation Using LATAS System

Irrigate daily, especially during hot and dry periods. Water for 30 to 60 minutes each day or as needed. Using drip irrigation or simple irrigation systems helps conserve water and improve growth.

6. Kulawa da Kwari da Cututtuka

A lura da kwari kamar su ciyawa da cututtuka kamar ∆ôura da ∆ôwayoyin cuta. Idan an ga tsire-tsire na da matsala, cire su don hana yaduwa. Yi amfani da magungunan kashe kwari da cututtuka masu dacewa.

6. Pest and Disease Management

Watch out for pests such as aphids and diseases like powdery mildew. Remove infected plants immediately to prevent spread. Use suitable pesticides and fungicides.

7. Girbi da Ajiye Latas

Girbi latas bayan wata 1 darabi zuwa 2, lokacin da ganyen suka yi kauri. A yanke ganye da kyau, a wanke su kafin a ajiye su a wuri mai sanyi da iska mai kyau don tsawon rayuwa.

7. Harvesting and Storage

Harvest kale after 6 to 8 weeks when leaves are mature. Cut leaves carefully, wash before storing in a cool, well-ventilated place to prolong shelf life.
