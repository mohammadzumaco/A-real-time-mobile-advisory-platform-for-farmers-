1. Land Preparation

Before planting soybeans, the land must be properly prepared. Soybeans grow best in soft, well-drained soil.

  • Clear the field of weeds and debris.
  • Use organic manure such as cow or chicken manure to enrich the soil.
  • Check soil conditions; avoid very hard or waterlogged soils.

2. Choosing Soybean Seeds

There are different varieties of soybeans. Choose seeds that suit your local climate and are resistant to pests and diseases.

3. Planting Soybeans

The best time to plant soybeans is during the rainy season when water is adequate.

  • Plant seeds at a depth of about 3-5 cm into the soil.
  • Leave a spacing of about 10-15 cm between plants and 30 cm between rows.
  • Plant in sunny areas to ensure good sunlight for growth.

4. Caring for Soybeans

After planting, proper care is essential to get a good yield:

  • Water the plants if rainfall is insufficient.
  • Remove weeds regularly to prevent competition for nutrients.
  • Control pests and diseases using appropriate pesticides or traditional methods.

5. Harvesting and Drying

Soybeans are ready for harvest 90 to 120 days after planting when the leaves begin to dry and pods are brown.

  • Harvest by hand or machine carefully.
  • Dry the soybeans in the shade or a well-ventilated area to avoid spoilage.
  • Store dried beans in a cool, dry place to prevent damage.

6. Uses of Soybeans

Soybeans are very useful in daily life:

  • Used to make foods like tofu, soy milk, and fried beans.
  • Feed for livestock.
  • Oil extracted is used for cooking and industrial products.
  • Help improve soil fertility by fixing nitrogen.

7. Storage Tips

To keep soybeans fresh and in good condition:

  • Store in a well-ventilated, dry place away from heat and moisture.
  • Use airtight containers or special bags for storage.
  • Avoid storing near strong-smelling substances.
  • Regularly check stored beans to remove any spoiled or pest-infested beans.

By following these steps, you can successfully farm soybeans and benefit from their many uses!
